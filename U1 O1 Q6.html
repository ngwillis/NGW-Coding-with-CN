Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

Just like Estates and Human Resources (HR), Information Technology (IT) can be considered as a central service in any organisation.

In a Pure Play [4] IT company, this can be the main activity that an organisation undertakes and can be used to describe companies of any size from large global to SMEs.

At the other extreme an individual working as a sole trader [5] may be dedicated exclusively to providing IT Services.
Family members, contractors or temporary staff me be providing accountancy and HR support for example.

Essentailly, the purpose of IT is to provide computer products or services.

These can range from computer, printer and communications hardware to a variety of software. Sometimes costs can be reduced when IT is provided as a “service”.
This means that you pay an annual fee on a regular basis and an external company will provide whatever you need.
