Unit 1 - Outcome 3 - Question 3

Develop solutions to improve own productivity using IT in digital projects

One solution is the mantra "one thing at a time".

During work periods i would mute slack and restrict use to breaks. Would also restrict work time for work activities.

I would stick to a 7.5 hour day with an mandatory 60 minute lunch break, fifteen minute breaks mid morning and mid afternoon as well as five minute breaks every hour.

Reviewing my progress at least one a day would help me achieve daily and weekly targets.

I would also subscribe to a limit number of newsletters and forums to keep in touch with professional and technical developments in IT and technology.
