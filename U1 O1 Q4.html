Describe the risks that might impact digital projects

Digital is now an outdated term originally used to describe the use of electronic or electrical equipment used to provide complete products and/or services. A digital watch is one example of a digital product. Just as the internet provides us with a myriad of services.

Digital project, depending on their complexity and scope may involve:

Hardware - clients, servers, mobile devices,

Services - World Wide Web [3]

Infrastructure - TheInternet [3]
