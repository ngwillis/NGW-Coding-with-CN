Unit 1 - Outcome 3 - Question 4

Describe how you would go about testing digital solutions

The first and worst test is developer testing.
Paradoxically the developer is the best and worst tester because I know my own software but may get a little foggy with prolonged testing.

Peer reviewing is also invaluable but only be available free on rare occasions.
This may be the best way to deal with technical issues.

User testing as always is the best form in my opinion because they will be the regular users
They have needs that must be met by my website.
They can provide valuable feedback, Especially those that are disabled.

I plan to include a feedback page on my website so anyone could complain, offer feedback and report issues.
